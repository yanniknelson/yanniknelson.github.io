LED Mask

For the halloween of 2017 I decided I wanted to make my own costume myself and wanted an element of this costume to be technological. Wrench from the game Watch_Dogs 2 provided the perfect oportunity for this. Wrench's mask has LED matrices in the eyes that change in order to match his emotional state or current thoughts. This meant I had to have an led display in the eyes thin enough to allow me to still wear the mask, a board to control those leds and some way of triggering the change of the design displayed.
